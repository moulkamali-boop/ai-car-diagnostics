Privacy Policy - AI Car Diagnostics

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

1. Data Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All diagnostic processes (camera images, microphone recordings, vibration sensor data, temperature estimates) are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded. We have no access to this data.

We use your device's LocalStorage only to save:

  • Remaining free diagnosis count
  • Diagnosis history (if you choose to save it)
  • Language preference
  • License key and email (if you purchased a pack) – stored only locally

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

2. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. The only exception is when you click "Buy" – you are redirected to Paddle's secure checkout page. Paddle collects payment information according to its own privacy policy. We never receive your credit card details.

3. Security

All communication between your browser and our Cloudflare Worker (which only verifies license keys) is encrypted via HTTPS.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this URL.

6. Contact Us

For privacy questions, email: mouloud.kamali123@gmail.com
